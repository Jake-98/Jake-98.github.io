About Me

Towards the end of my junior year in college, a friend showed me some of the projects he was creating through his computer science courses. I was instantly hooked by the possibility of solving problems through software. Shortly after that, I switched my major to integrative studies to take the fundamental courses in the programming and mathematics required to pursue a career in software development. However, I am mostly a self-taught devloper and have been programming a little over a year now. I also earned the full-stack developer certification offered through freeCodeCamp.

My programming skills include knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, Python, and many other libraries and frameworks. In addition to this, my skills in photoshop and design will save you from having to hire a UI designer. Plus, I know the code to turn a design into a fully functional web page. Outside of coding, I have exceptional written and verbal communication skills in the languages of English and German. I would be a great addition to your team as I have the drive to succeed in whatever task I take on and the determination to see it through to the end.

When I am not writing code, I enjoy spending time away from the computer to pursue my interests in weightlifting, hiking, camping, running, and music. I also enjoy learning about mathematics, specifically fractals and cymatics. Currently, I am working on skills and projects in the areas of cybersecurity and cloud computing.
