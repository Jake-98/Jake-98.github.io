About Me

Towards the end of my junior year in college, a friend showed me some of the projects he was creating through his computer science courses. I was hooked by the potential IT and software has to change the world. Shortly after that, I switched my major to integrative studies and took courses in the programming and mathematics required to pursue a career in cyber security. I am mostly self-taught when it comes to troubleshooting computer hardware and networking devices. I am a self-taught developer as well.

Since dial-up internet and Windows 98, computers have been part of my life as far back as I can remember. I first began tinkering with one of my Grandpa's old laptops in elementary school and continue to do so today to maximum the efficiency of my computers and network. I also regularly help friends and family with any tech-related problems. My programming skills include knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and many other libraries and frameworks. Outside of technology, I have exceptional written and verbal communication skills in the languages of English and German. I would be a great addition to your team as I have the drive to succeed in whatever task I take on and the determination to see it through to the end.

When I am not writing code or solving IT problems, I enjoy spending time away from the computer to pursue my interests in weightlifting, hiking, camping, and music. I also enjoy learning about mathematics, specifically fractals and cymatics. Currently, I am working on obtaining both the CompTIA Network+ and Security+ certifications and plan to do so within the next couple months.
